Kale salad with rye kernels

Ingredients

150 g rye kernels or whole grain rice

150 g of fresh kale leaves

150 g of edamame beans

1 apple (about 100 g)

1 red onion (about 100 g)

1 tsp. oil

½ tsp. smoked paprika

1 pinch of salt

50 g almonds

50 g salad with a maximum of 17% fat

Marinade

1½ tbsp. oil

4 tbsp. apple vinegar

2 tsp. honey

salt, pepper

Method

Boil rye kernels or whole grain rice as directed on the packaging and cool them.

Remove the stick from the kale and chop the cabbage very finely.

Pour boiling water over the beans and cool them.

Cut the apple into thin slices.

Halve the onion and cut it into thin strips.

Heat the oil - do not smoke it - and add the pepper, salt and almonds. Shake them for a few minutes, so the pepper spreads to the almonds. Cool them down.

Mix all ingredients for the marinade.

Mix rice or grains together with vegetables and salad and turn in marinade.

Chop the almonds roughly and spread them over the salad just before serving.
