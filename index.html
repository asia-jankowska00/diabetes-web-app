Kale salad with rye kernels

(4 Servings)

Ingredients

150 g rye kernels or whole grain rice

150 g of fresh kale leaves

150 g of edamame beans

1 apple (about 100 g)

1 red onion (about 100 g)

1 tsp. oil

½ tsp. smoked paprika

1 pinch of salt

50 g almonds

50 g salad with a maximum of 17% fat

Marinade

1½ tbsp. oil

4 tbsp. apple vinegar

2 tsp. honey

salt, pepper

Method

Boil rye kernels or whole grain rice as directed on the packaging and cool them.

Remove the stick from the kale and chop the cabbage very finely.

Pour boiling water over the beans and cool them.

Cut the apple into thin slices.

Halve the onion and cut it into thin strips.

Heat the oil - do not smoke it - and add the pepper, salt and almonds. Shake them for a few minutes, so the pepper spreads to the almonds. Cool them down.

Mix all ingredients for the marinade.

Mix rice or grains together with vegetables and salad and turn in marinade.

Chop the almonds roughly and spread them over the salad just before serving.

Nutricion Facts

Salad and accessories, Vegetarian, Cold dishes Pr. person: Energy 1625 kJ, Carbohydrate 44 g, Fat 16 g, Carbohydrate per 100 g 20 g

Tomato Soup

(1 Servings)

Ingredients

½ leeks (about 60 g)

1 small carrot (about 50 g)

1 slice of cabbage (about 200 g)

75 g minced beef with a maximum of 10% fat

1 tsp. liquid fat

2½ dl vegetable broth

½ ds. peeled tomatoes (200 g)

½ tbsp. tomato paste, preferably concentrate

½ tsp. mild pepper

¼ tsp. dried basil

salt, pepper

Marinade

1½ tbsp. oil

4 tbsp. apple vinegar

2 tsp. honey

salt, pepper

Serving size

1 Person

Method

Cut the leek into thin rings. Cut the carrot into small cubes. Cut the cabbage finely.

Brown the meat in the fat. Add the vegetables and sweat them. Add the remaining ingredients and cook the soup for 10-15 minutes. Taste the soup for.

Serving

Serve tomato soup with whole grain bread.

Alternative

You can replace the carrot or some of the cabbage with celery.

Nutricion Facts

Pr. person: Energy 1450 kJ, Carbohydrate 25 g, Fat 14 g, Carbohydrate per 100 g 3 g

Mini pizza

(4 Servings)

Ingredients

3½ dl water

½ hp. yeast (25 g)

2 tsp. oil, like olive oil

1 tsp. salt

100 g white wheat flour

150 g graham flour (2½ dl)

ca. 250 g wheat flour (6 dl)

Fill

150 g pastrami in slices

5 cherry tomatoes (75 g)

1 small green pepper (100 g)

Garnish

100 g of grated cheese with a maximum of 17% fat

dried oregano

Tomato Sauce

1 small onion (50 g)

1 carrot (75 g)

1 clove of garlic

1 tsp. oil

1 ds. peeled tomatoes (400 g)

2 tsp. dried oregano

salt, pepper

Serving size

20 pcs.

Method

Strain the water and stir the yeast into it. Add the other ingredients to the dough - hold back some of the wheat flour.

Knead the dough well. Cover the dough and allow it to rise slightly for approx. 45 min.

Tomato Sauce

Arrow the onion and finely chop. Peel the carrot and finely grate. Press the garlic.

Put the oil in a pan and lightly heat it up. Add the vegetables and lightly sweat them.

Add the peeled tomatoes and oregano, and simmer the sauce for approx. 20 min. without a lid.

Cool the sauce lightly and season with salt and pepper.

Fill

Cut the pastrami into strips. Cut the cherry tomatoes into slices. Cut the peppers into cubes.

Toss the dough and divide it into 20 pieces and roll it into buns.

Roll out each bun to small pizza bottoms. Distribute the tomato sauce on the bottoms.

Fill the pizzas and sprinkle cheese and oregano on.

Bake the mini pizzas at 200ºC for approx. 15 min. on middle oven rack. Cool the pizzas on a baking dish.

Serving

Eat the mini pizzas hot as a lunch dish with vegetables or cold in the lunch box.

Alternative

Pastrami can be replaced by chicken teas, ham strips, tuna or shrimp. Other vegetables can be used.

Nutrition Facts

Packed lunch and dinner, Hot dishes, Snack and snack

Pr. pcs .: Energy 550 kJ, Carbohydrate 19 g, Fat 3 g, Carbohydrate per 100 g 22 g

Chicken friezes with coconut

(2 Servings)

Ingredients

1 onion (55 g)

1 small carrot (45 g)

50 g of edamame beans without pods

200 minced chicken meat maximum 10% fat

½ tsp. salt

1 egg

½ dl fine rolled oatmeal (20 g)

½ dl coconut flour (20 g)

1-2 tsp. lime or lemon juice

Possibly. 1 tbsp. milk with a maximum of 0.7% fat

Pepper

2 tsp. liquid fat for frying

Serving size

10 pcs.

Method

Grate onion and carrot. Pour boiling water over the edamame beans. Roughly chop them. Stir the chicken meat with salt and add the egg.

Add all the ingredients except the milk to the farce and stir well. If milk is too firm, add milk. The father benefits from pulling in the refrigerator for approx. ½ hour.

Form 10 meatballs, and fry them in the fat for 5-6 minutes. on each side until they are cooked through. Remove the meatballs from the pan when fried.

Serving

Serve Chicken Frikadella with coconut for potatoes, rice or pasta salad and vegetables. You can use the rest of the meatballs as a topping.

Variation

Use other vegetables after the season eg. parsley root, parsnip, celeriac, turnip, spring onion.

Use minced pork or veal instead of chicken.

Nutrition Facts

Pr. pcs .: Energy 325 kJ, Carbohydrate 3 g, Fat 5 g, Carbohydrate per 100 g 7 g

Spandauer with cream and jam

(4 Servings)

Ingredients

Dough

2½ dl milk with a maximum of 0.7% fat

1 hp yeast (50 g)

1 egg

50 g liquid margarine

2 tbsp. sugar

¼ tsp. salt

150 g oatmeal, finely rolled (5 dl)

ca. 350 g wheat flour (approx. 5½ dl)

Cream

1 egg

1 tbsp. corn starch

2 parts milk with a maximum of 0.7% fat

1 tsp. vanilla sugar

sweetener equivalent to 20-25 g of sugar, preferably liquid

Fill

100 g of jam without added sugar eg. Strawberry

Garnish

Glaze possibly. colored, melted chocolate or icing

Serving size

16 pcs.

Method

Cream (cook this first)

Whisk eggs, cornstarch, milk and vanilla sugar together in a thick-bottomed pan. Bring the mixture to a boil and cook for 1-2 minutes while whipping.

Add sweetener, taste and cool cream, covered, in refrigerator.

Dough

Heat the milk until hand-warm and dissolve the yeast in it. Add eggs, margarine, sugar, salt, oatmeal and most of the wheat flour

Knead the dough well and add any. the last wheat flour until the dough is released.

Let the dough rest for approx. 5 min.

Divide the dough into 16 equal parts and roll it into buns. Press or roll with a rolling pin buns flat (about 10 cm in diameter) and dispense 1 tbsp. cream and 1 tsp. jam on each.

Fold the dough together against the filling and place the buns on a baking sheet with baking paper. Allow the buns to rise for approx. 15 min.

Bake them at 200⁰ C for approx. 15 min. on the middle or second upper furnace groove. Cool them on a baking rack.

If you want to decorate the lobster balls, you can melt approx. 25 g of chocolate in a small plastic microwave oven bag. Cut a very small corner of the bag and sprinkle the chocolate on the buns.

You can also make icing of icing and boiling water and decorate it. Color the glaze with fruit color. Alternatively, sprinkle icing over the buns.

Variation

Replace jam with 50 g of dark or light chocolate. You can also use raisins to fill with the cream

Tip

You can make the cream the day before you bake pie buns. Store in a refrigerator.

Nutrition Facts

Pr. pcs .: Energy 725 kJ, Carbohydrate 27 g, Fat 5 g, Carbohydrate per 100 g 36 g

Hot chocolate

(4 Servings)

Ingredients

1 DL water

25 g of dark chocolate with at least 70% cocoa

25 g of cocoa

7 parts milk with a maximum of 0.7% fat

sweetener equivalent to 40-50 g sugar

Garnish

1 dl milk with a maximum of 0.7% fat

Serving size

4 People

Method

Heat the water in a saucepan and melt the chocolate in it. Beat the cocoa powder into the mixture.

Add the milk and warm to the boil. Remove from the heat and season with sweetener.

Heat the milk for decoration e.g. in microwave and foam it up with a milk frother.

Serving

Garnish the cocoa with milk foam and serve immediately for a snack eg. for fasteners.

Tip

You can add cinnamon, vanilla sugar, chopped cardamom or chopped cloves to vary the taste.

Nutrition Facts

Pr. person: Energy 570 kJ, Carbohydrate 11 g, Fat 5 g, Carbohydrate per 100 g 6 g
