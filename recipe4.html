Recipes

Chicken friezes with coconut

Ingredients

1 onion (55 g)

1 small carrot (45 g)

50 g of edamame beans without pods

200 minced chicken meat maximum 10% fat

½ tsp. salt

1 egg

½ dl fine rolled oatmeal (20 g)

½ dl coconut flour (20 g)

1-2 tsp. lime or lemon juice

Possibly. 1 tbsp. milk with a maximum of 0.7% fat

Pepper

2 tsp. liquid fat for frying

Serving size

10 pcs.

Method

Grate onion and carrot. Pour boiling water over the edamame beans. Roughly chop them. Stir the chicken meat with salt and add the egg.

Add all the ingredients except the milk to the farce and stir well. If milk is too firm, add milk. The father benefits from pulling in the refrigerator for approx. ½ hour.

Form 10 meatballs, and fry them in the fat for 5-6 minutes. on each side until they are cooked through. Remove the meatballs from the pan when fried.

Serving

Serve Chicken Frikadella with coconut for potatoes, rice or pasta salad and vegetables. You can use the rest of the meatballs as a topping.

Variation

Use other vegetables after the season eg. parsley root, parsnip, celeriac, turnip, spring onion.

Use minced pork or veal instead of chicken.

Nutrition Facts

Pr. pcs .: Energy 325 kJ, Carbohydrate 3 g, Fat 5 g, Carbohydrate per 100 g 7 g
